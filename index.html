Privacy Policy

FluxBlocks · Effective date: April 21, 2025

FluxBlocks ("the app") is operated by Bumblepop Studios. This policy explains what data is collected when you use the app and how it is used.

Information Collected

The app itself does not collect or store any personal information. However, the third-party services it uses may collect data as described below.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of FluxBlocks displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID) and other non-personal information to serve relevant ads. In regions where consent is required (such as the EEA and UK), you will be shown a consent dialog before any ads are loaded.

You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

If you make a purchase (such as Remove Ads), the transaction is handled by the App Store or Google Play. RevenueCat is used to verify and manage entitlements. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase history to provide this service. No payment details are ever seen or stored by the app.

RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data We Do Not Collect

FluxBlocks does not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other personal data.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the effective date above will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
